
Open Command Prompt in that folder.

Run:

for %i in (*.png) do cwebp "%i" -q 80 -o "%~ni.webp"

That converts:

frame_0001.png → frame_0001.webp
frame_0002.png → frame_0002.webp
...




Recommended Quality Settings

Use:

-q 75 to 85

Typical sweet spot:

-q 80

Good quality + much smaller files.







If You Want Transparency (Alpha)

Just use:

for %i in (*.png) do cwebp "%i" -q 80 -alpha_q 100 -o "%~ni.webp"

That preserves:

transparent backgrounds
glow edges
smooth anti-aliasing










Even Better — Multi-threaded Batch (Much Faster)

If conversion is slow, use:

for %i in (*.png) do cwebp "%i" -q 80 -mt -o "%~ni.webp"

The -mt flag uses multiple CPU cores.

Important for 2000 files.
